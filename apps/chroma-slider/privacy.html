Chroma Slider — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 11, 2026

Shoogar Soft Inc. ("we", "us") operates the Chroma Slider mobile app ("the App"). This page explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

Data we collect directly

Chroma Slider does not require an account, does not use a server backend, and does not collect or transmit personal information. Your game progress (achievements, stage completion, settings) is stored only on your device using local storage, and is never sent to us. You can clear this data at any time from Settings > Reset Progress, or by uninstalling the App.

Data collected by advertising

Chroma Slider shows ads served through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as the advertising ID), general device/app information, and approximate location, to serve and measure ads. This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. See Google's own disclosures for details:

  • How Google uses data
  • Google Privacy Policy

Children's privacy

Chroma Slider is not directed at children under 13 and is not part of Google Play's "Designed for Families" program.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the App changes. Continued use of the App after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
